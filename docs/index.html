About Us

Welcome to the Flight Strip Manager! This website is designed to help pilots manage their flight strips, track flight details, and ensure their flight plans are well-organized.

Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for pilots to organize their flight details, helping them keep track of routes, times, and important data. Fly smart, fly safe, and let us handle the planning.

We want to help pilots in flight simulators like Xplane and Microsoft Flight Simulator getting their flight plans done easily and efficiently. We will have a section with various airports, charts and METARS from all over the world.
